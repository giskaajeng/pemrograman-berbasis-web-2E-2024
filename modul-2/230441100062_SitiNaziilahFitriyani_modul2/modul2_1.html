Indonesia

Indonesia is a country that is very rich in natural beauty and diverse culture.
Indonesian people from various tribes, religions, races and different groups in social,
and state life are the central point of development subjects and the basic strength of existing tourism development.
The diversity of Indonesia's natural resources owned by each region is an important capital for increasing economic growth
and community welfare. Indonesia is known to have stunning natural beauty. Starting from exotic beaches,
majestic mountains, to pristine forests. However, behind the well-known natural beauty, there are also hidden natural tourist charms
that are rarely known by many people. This hidden natural tourist attraction has beauty that is no less amazing, but is often still rarely touched by tourists

Mount Bromo

Mount Bromo is famous as a major tourist attraction in East Java. As a tourist attraction, Bromo is interesting because of its status as an active volcano. Mount Bromo is included in the Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park area.

Gili Trawangan

Gili Trawangan is a small island located in Lombok, West Nusa Tenggara. This island has an area of around 338 hectares and is one of the favorite tourist destinations in Indonesia. Gili Trawangan Lombok is famous for its natural underwater beauty, stunning white sand beaches, and various interesting activities that tourists can enjoy

Pink Beach

One of the popular tourist attractions known among tourists today is Pink Beach. Pink Beach is an exotic beach that has beautiful views with pink sand. This beach is also known as Red Beach on Komodo Island
